## end customers
individuals and buinesses that take out insurance cover

## advisors
third parties that advise and act on behalf of individuals seeking personal (e.g. life, casualty, travel) insurance

## brokers
third parties that advise and act on behalf of businesses seeking commerical (e.g. property, worker compensation) insurance

## underwriters
work for the business ard make risk-based decisions to accept or decline (and set potential loadings or exclusions) on cover for customers

## support staff
help underwriters by processing quotation requests and extracting, structuring and requesting required information.
