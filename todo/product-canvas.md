


## Product Overview

We're building A system that makes it possible to get things that matter done  in the Tech industry because current tools create disconnected silos of information

## Product Vision
To empower software engineers by seamlessly connecting code, documentation, and collaboration, fostering innovation and accelerating impactful development.

## North Star Metric
Number of strategic decisions informed by NB insights.

## Product Why
We believe in empowering tech professionals by dismantling information silos, enabling them to accelerate innovation and deliver impactful solutions with unparalleled speed and clarity.
NB intelligently unifies disparate data, proactively surfaces critical insights, and streamlines complex workflows, fostering seamless collaboration and accelerating impactful innovation for engineers.
NB is an intelligent knowledge orchestration platform that unifies enterprise data, automates discovery, and links information to drive strategic execution for faster decision-making.

## Problem
Fragmented data silos hinder knowledge workers, slowing down critical decision-making and innovation.
Inaccessible and disparate information sources create operational inefficiencies and increase business risk.
Current tools fail to connect information, leading to lost productivity and delayed strategic initiatives.
Lack of unified knowledge management impedes effective collaboration and problem-solving in tech teams.

## Solution
NB is an intelligent knowledge orchestration platform unifying enterprise data into a single, actionable intelligence layer. Leveraging advanced semantic analysis and contextual linking, it surfaces critical insights like emerging trends or compliance risks, automates knowledge discovery, and streamlines complex workflows. This empowers users to accelerate strategic execution, break down silos, and drive measurable business outcomes.

## Product Principles
Unify knowledge, break down silos. Connect disparate data to make information universally accessible and actionable, maximizing collective understanding and collaboration.
Empower intelligent action. Deliver actionable insights that enable informed decisions, driving efficiency and accelerating strategic execution for our users.
Prioritize user-centric design. Create intuitive experiences that reduce friction and cognitive load, enhancing the knowledge worker's productivity and satisfaction.
Foster continuous learning and adaptation. Evolve with user needs, leveraging AI to proactively surface relevant information and drive ongoing discovery.
Ensure data integrity and security. Safeguard information with robust security and reliability, building trust through discoverable and dependable knowledge.
